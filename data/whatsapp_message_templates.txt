WhatsApp Message Templates
===========================

These templates can be used with the batch messaging system.
Use {placeholders} that match column names in your CSV file.

BUSINESS TEMPLATES:
-------------------

1. Order Confirmation:
Hi {name}, your order #{order_id} has been confirmed! Expected delivery: {delivery_date}. Thank you for shopping with us!

2. Appointment Reminder:
Hello {name}, this is a reminder for your appointment on {date} at {time}. Location: {location}. See you soon!

3. Payment Reminder:
Dear {name}, your payment of {amount} for {service} is due on {due_date}. Please pay at your earliest convenience.

4. Product Launch:
Hi {name}! We're excited to announce our new {product}! Special launch price: {price}. Order now at {link}

5. Event Invitation:
Hello {name}! You're invited to {event_name} on {date} at {venue}. RSVP by {rsvp_date}. Hope to see you there!

6. Shipping Update:
Hi {name}, great news! Your order #{order_id} has been shipped and will arrive by {delivery_date}. Track: {tracking_link}

7. Survey Request:
Hello {name}, we value your feedback! Please take 2 minutes to complete our survey: {survey_link}. Thank you!

8. Discount Offer:
Hi {name}! Exclusive offer just for you: {discount}% off on {product}. Use code: {promo_code}. Valid until {expiry_date}

CUSTOMER SERVICE TEMPLATES:
---------------------------

1. Support Ticket Resolved:
Hi {name}, your support ticket #{ticket_id} has been resolved. Please let us know if you need further assistance.

2. Service Downtime Notice:
Dear {name}, our {service} will be under maintenance on {date} from {start_time} to {end_time}. We apologize for any inconvenience.

3. Account Update:
Hello {name}, your {account_type} account has been successfully updated. Changes will take effect from {effective_date}.

HOW TO USE:
-----------

1. Create a CSV file with required columns (e.g., name, order_id, date)
2. Choose or customize a template above
3. Run: python scripts/whatsapp_batch.py send your_file.csv --template "Your template text here"

Example:
python scripts/whatsapp_batch.py send customers.csv --template "Hi {name}, your order #{order_id} is ready!"

For more help:
python scripts/whatsapp_batch.py --help
